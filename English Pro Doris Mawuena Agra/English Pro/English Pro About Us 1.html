About Us

EnglishPro is the place where real learning takes place. Join several users across the globe to explore resources, take tests, and improve upon challenging areas.

What We Do

EnglishPRO offers resources and tests to improve your reading skills. Read books, and listen to stories written by African Children. Take tests to identify what you find easy or challenging.

Why We Do what We Do

Our passion is fueled by the joy that you will experience when you break a language barrier. Using our resources ensures a smooth learning process.

Who We Do This For

Our resources are targeted at children at the basic level of education, including those who may not have stepped in any classroom before. The best time to solve a problem is early.
